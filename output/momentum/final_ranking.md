## Risk lens & leverage-safety note

**Sector concentration is the list's biggest structural risk: 6 of 10 names (AVGO, MU, NVDA, SNDK, CDNS, and arguably CAT's genset book) are one AI-capex trade.** A single hyperscaler capex-digestion announcement marks the whole cluster down together — diversification across this list is partly an illusion. Within the list: SNDK and MU are the highest-volatility names (memory has repeatedly drawn down 50-70% peak-to-trough when cycles turn; SNDK's parabolic chart makes it the single most drawdown-exposed); NVDA/AVGO are high-beta but cash-rich; HWM/GE/LLY/CDNS are the steadier compounders; CAT and FCX are cyclicals wearing AI hats. Education, not advice: high-beta cyclicals like memory can fall 50%+ fast, which is brutal and potentially account-ending with margin — any use of leverage on names like SNDK/MU/NVDA should be tempered by that drawdown reality, and no specific leverage ratio or position size is recommended here.
