## Per-ticker notes

**MU** — Cleanest pure-shortage: HBM sold out & contracted before production, FQ3 (reports Jun 24) record ~$33.5B / ~81% GM / ~$19 EPS. 3-firm un-in-sourceable oligopoly but it's the #3, so allocation can shift to SK Hynix/Samsung. Main enemy = memory cyclicality. Reports tomorrow.

**SNDK** — Highest-octane / highest-beta memory: NAND "silent squeeze," 2026 sold out, contract prices +~38% Q1. But #5 of 5, tied to Kioxia JV; parabolic stock (spun from WDC early 2025, +~500% in 2026). Most operating leverage AND most cyclical downside.

**NVDA** — #1 and most demand-constrained, but weakest irreplaceability: its own customers (Google TPU, AWS Trainium, Meta MTIA, via AVGO) are in-sourcing; ASIC shipments growing ~45% vs ~16% merchant GPU. CUDA software moat strong; hardware increasingly bypassable. Guidance assumes zero China.

**AVGO** — The in-sourcing *vehicle* — builds the ASICs hyperscalers use to escape NVDA. Hardest contracted backlog (~$162B). Risk = customer concentration + a client taking design fully in-house (Google dual-sourcing TPU v8 w/ MediaTek). Fell ~15% on Q2 print because FY26 AI target not raised.

**LRCX** — Best moat/shortage balance among semicap (8/8): #1 etch, >90% HAR etch, levered to HBM+NAND-conversion+GAA. Risk: China 34% of revenue, stock +114% YTD.

**KLAC** — Widest moat in the whole set (9): ~56-63% process-control, ~7x nearest rival, qualification-locked, complexity-driven growth. Lower beta/explosive upside; premium valuation; DRAM component cost headwind.

**AMAT** — Broadest WFE franchise but most China/export-control encumbered (~$600M FY26 hit, $253M settlement), stock +141%. Least asymmetric semicap.

**APH** — Loudest order book (+78% record bookings, 1.24 B:B) but thinnest moat (5): copper→optical substitution is existential (NVDA co-packaged optics, $2B Lumentum stake), fragmented connector market (TE/Molex). CCS acquisition closed Jan 2026.

**HWM** — Purest irreplaceable choke point (9/9): >90% of structural/rotating aero-engine component types, single-crystal casting, certification lock-in. But richest multiple in set (~64x P/E); avg PT ~$305 ≈ only ~10% above price — explosion largely already happened.

**GE** — Highest-quality annuity: $211B RPO, ~70% aftermarket, CFM >60% narrowbody, un-disintermediable type-certified engines. But too big to 5-bag; ~44x fwd P/E; didn't raise guidance on Q1 beat.

**CAT** — Biggest acute demand shock + biggest backlog jump (+79% to $63B) on AI-datacenter power. But lowest moat of industrials (6): gensets fungible/multi-sourced, substitution from turbines/Wärtsilä; most cyclical (mining/construction ~2/3); trades ~49-51x, above Street targets.

**LLY** — Genuine GLP-1 demand + freshly-approved oral Foundayo uncorks a far bigger TAM (no cold chain). #1 incretins, widening vs Novo. Patented molecule can't be in-sourced. But ~30-35x fwd P/E; IRA pricing + Novo oral Wegovy are lateral risks.

**GOOGL** — Most "MU-like" of the non-semis: explicitly compute-constrained, $462B cloud backlog ~doubled QoQ, owns full silicon(TPU)→model(Gemini)→cloud stack. Cheapest vs growth of its batch. Overhang = antitrust remedies (search/AdX) + $190B capex intensity + AI-search self-cannibalization.

**NEM** — Weakest fit: price-taking commodity miner, no backlog, no pricing power (irrepl. 3). Record cash but single-variable gold-price risk; gold already pulled back ~16% from Jan peak. Included for breadth only.
