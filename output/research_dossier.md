**GE (8)** — Cleanest "shortage = pricing power": $211.3B RPO, $170B commercial services; spare-parts orders +30%, LEAP shop visits +50%, >95% of Q2 spares already in backlog. LEAP ~76% of A320neo wins; sole engine on 737 MAX; Pratt GTF durability woes feed share. Risk: capacity-constrained deliveries; airline-cycle exposure; rich valuation.
